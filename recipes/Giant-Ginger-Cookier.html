Giant Ginger Cookies

General Information

Prep: 40 minutes

Bake: 12 min per batch at 350 F

Servings: 48 cookies

Ingredients

  • 4 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 1/2 ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cup shortening
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup molasses
  • 3/4 cup coarse sugar or granulated sugar

Bakeware

  • medium mixing bowl
  • large mixing bowl
  • electric mixer
  • cookie sheet
  • wooden spoon
  • wire rack

How to Make Giant Ginger Cookies

Step by Step

  1. Pre heat the oven to 350 F. In the medium mixing bowl, stir the ginger, flour, cinnamon, cloves, and salt. Set the mixture aside

  2. In the large mixing bowl, beat the shortening with the electric mixer on low for 30 seconds. Gradually add the 2 cups of granulated sugar. Beat until combined, scarping sides of the bowl occasionally. Beat in the eggs and molasses. Beat in as much of the flour mixture as you can with mixer. Using the wooden spoon, stir any remaining flour mixture.

  3. Shape the dough into 2-inch balls using a 1/4 cup dough. Roll the balls 3/4 cup of additional coarse or granulated sugar. Place about 2 1/2 inches apart on an ungreased cookie sheet.

  4. Bake for 12 to 14 minutes or until the cookies are light brown and puffed. Cool on cookie sheet for 2 minutes. Transfer to a wirerack and cool.

Storage

  1. Layer the cookies between sheets of wax paper in an airtight container; cover.

  2. Store at room temperature for up to 3 days or in the freezer for up to 3 months.
